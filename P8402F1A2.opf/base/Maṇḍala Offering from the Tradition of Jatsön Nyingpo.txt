Maṇḍala Offering
from the Tradition of Jatsön Nyingpo

om ah hung
Oṃ āḥ hūṃ.
chöying mandal rigpé gyenpa di
The maṇḍala of absolute space, ornamented by rigpa,
lama sangye chö kyi ku la bul
I offer to the guru, the dharmakāya buddha!
rang lü mandal wangpö gyenpa di
The maṇḍala of my own body, ornamented by the faculties,
lama longchö dzokpé ku la bul
I offer to the saṃbhogakāya guru of perfect enjoyment!
rinchen mandal ser yü gyenpa di
The precious maṇḍala, ornamented with gold and turquoise,
lama tukjé trulpé ku la bul
I offer to the compassionate nirmāṇakāya guru!
guru ratna mandal kamnirya tayami
Guru ratna maṇḍalakaṃ niryātayāmi
